Welcome to Play My Game

This game is about a Tibetan boy named Tenzin, who, after fleeing to Dharamsala and learning to code, creates a website called "Voices of Tibet", a site where Tibetans can share stories so that their homeland is never forgotten.

This is a fun game where you must use the arrow buttons to move Tenzin so that he may collect his friends, who help him create his website, his chip, which holds all the data that allows him to visit the site, the wires to ensure his computor is working and stable, and a computor, so that he may create and edit his site.
